A Single Scroll Through the Ming

One page, one continuous stage. As the timeline advances, the main frame crossfades through the dynasty's major people and events: Zhu Yuanzhang, the Hongwu order, Yongle Beijing, Zheng He's fleets, the Tumu crisis, Qi Jiguang, Zhang Juzheng, Wanli court drift, the late-Ming urban world, and the fall of 1644.
