Front-page narrative

What Official History Displays First

The site deliberately begins with visual abundance: power is easy to browse, easy to admire, and richly indexed.

Portraits

Monarchs, ministers, reformers, commanders rendered as complete and legible subjects.

Wars

Campaign maps and directives elevate strategy while flattening the ordinary dead into totals.

Reforms

Policy as authored vision, detached from the labor required to execute it.

Prosperity

"Golden ages" appear as finished surfaces, not as extraction, discipline, and work.
